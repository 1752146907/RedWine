Questions & Answers

> How do I pay ?

You pay for all the months you subscribe to at the moment you order.

> How can I reorder the wines in the box?

You can reorder the wines you liked with a 10% discount. Just go on the page of the (You canscan the QR code on the Tasting Guide you received) and you’ll see the price will automatically be different for you.

> My subscription ends next month, how can I subscribe again?

You can subscribe again by ordering a new duration (3, 6 or 12 months). Our team will recognize your account and will extend your duration.

> How can I offer this subscription?

You can offer it by choosing the address of your friend and by clicking on the «gift option» in the check out page.

> When will my order arrive?

- For your first box: if you subscribe before the 15th of the month, your box will leave the warehouse on the 15th of the month. If you subscribe on the 15th of the month or after, your box will leave the day after your order.

- For your next months boxes: your next boxes will leave the warehouse each month on the 15th.

> How can we cancel the subscription?

You can cancel your subscription by calling the customer service : 400-885-1289 or by mail :mei.li@auchan.com.cn You can cancel the months for which your box has not left the warehouse (each box leaves on the 15th of the month).

> Can I get a refund if I cancel?

You can get a refund for the month for which you box has not left the warehouse. If you chose 6 or 12 months, you get a 5% or 10% discount. In this case, if you cancel your subscription before the end of the 6 or 12 months, we will deduct from the refund the duration discount. Indeed, this duration discounts (5%, 10%) is only valid if you keep the subscription until the end of the duration.
